# Product Roadmap: The OVERLORD Strategy

## Phase 1: Genesis (Month 1-3)
- Internal Alpha with 50 invited creators.
- Focus: Refining the AI Image-to-Text extraction.
- Outcome: Stable asset uploader and basic generator.

## Phase 2: Synthesis (Month 4-6)
- Public Beta launch.
- Focus: "Chat-to-Refine" feature (NLP interface for design changes).
- Outcome: 10,000 active portfolios.

## Phase 3: Expansion (Month 7-12)
- Focus: Marketplace for AI-Design "Personalities".
- Focus: Direct integration with LinkedIn and Behance.
- Outcome: The industry standard for creative digital resumes.

## Phase 4: Autonomy (Year 2+)
- Focus: Dynamic portfolios that update themselves by watching a creator's social media or cloud folders.
- Outcome: Zero-maintenance digital presence.
